Wednesday 5.29.19

Adventure Day

I Bike Tuscany: Ali & Jake did this tour on their trip last year. It’s an amazing (and not so difficult) bike and wine tasting tour through the Chianti towns. One of the most beautiful areas in Tuscany! They also have other tours you can look into.

Cooking Class at Badia a Coltibuono: Recently featured in a Netflix special. They offer a half day course that begins at 10:30.

Tenuta Quadrifoglio: Maybe you want to relax by the pool and stay on the property. They offer wine tastings, cooking classes & massages.

Other nearby towns to explore:

Chianti Region: 45-1 hour from Tenuta Quadrifoglio. Some of our family favorites include Radda in Chianti, Castellina in Chianti, & Greve in Chianti

Sienna: 30 minutes from Tenuta Quadrifoglio. Larger town in Tuscany. Many places to eat, shop and tour.
