Ali-Marie & Jake

Are getting Marrried!

... In Italy!

The Couple



The first time Ali and Jake met each other was at a twins game, and they said hi, but Jake was super shy and they didn't talk anymore than that. Then over a year later Jake and "the uptown boys" hosted a Friendsgiving and Ali met up with them after the dinner. That was the first time Jake finally talked to Ali. From that point on they kept finding themselves talking, spending time together and realizing how much they had in common. Finally on New Years Eve, Jake asked Ali out on a date and she said yes!

They dated for a year and half. Jake was Ali's date in her sister Ashley's wedding. Ali was Jake's date in his sister Megan's wedding. Then Jake was Ali's date again in her brother Andrew's wedding! A lot of important and exciting weddings in a year. (Thank you parents!). In May of 2018, they took a trip to Europe, where they traveled around Italy, Switzerland, and France. When they got to Lake Como, Italy, after a strenuous drive, Jake had found the perfect spot. It's the picture above, a pergola on Lake Como. Jake proposed to Ali under the vines and they got engaged!

Ali and Jake both live in Northeast Minneapolis. Jake is a Software Engineer at SportsEngine and Ali is a Freelance Photography Producer. They are extremely excited to get married and cannot wait for their special day. Whether you will celebrate with them in Tuscany, Minnesota, or anywhere in between, it means so much to them that you are involved in their lives and their wedding.
